About Derrick

My name is Derrick Lieu. I'm a first-generation Vietnamese-Chinese American and a 4th year Human-Computer Interaction major at UC San Diego. I really enjoy learning about design principles and tools (through youtube tutorials mostly) and aspire to become a visual designer who specializes in all aspects branding/rebranding. Truth be told, outside of design and school, I am a complete cinephile (having watched over 400 films, over half of that in the last two years). My favorite movies are In the Mood for Love , Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind , and Synedoche, New York although I have a love for many more. Along with film, I love fashion, specifically streetwear because it acts as perfect outlet of expression and creativity for me. If you share a passion with design, film, or streetwear, let's chat! :)
